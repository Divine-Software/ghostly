Ghostly

A divine template/print formatter engine

Get Started

Print formatting/PDF generator

Ghostly is perfect for generating invoces, rich sales reports or any other kind of printable documents. Being powered by a modern web browser, its layout capabilities are second to none.

Generate beautiful emails

Each template can produce several output formats, such as plain text, HTML, PDF, or even PNG and JPEG images. Additionally, a template may generate any number of secondary results, such as email attachments or CSV/spreadsheet documents.

Fully programmable templates

Ghostly templates are powered by HTML, CSS and JavaScript/TypeScript. Roll your own template engine, or use Handlebars, Angular, React, Stencil ... Ghostly templates can contain complex logic and even query remote databases or web services during rendering.
